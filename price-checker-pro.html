Project Overview

The problem I solved.

Grocery prices for the same product can vary by 20–40% between stores in the same area. Most shoppers have no idea — they just pay whatever's on the shelf. Price Match Paradise fixes this.

I built a mobile app that lets shoppers scan any barcode in the aisle, instantly see the same item's price at 50+ nearby stores, and show the screen at checkout to get a price match on the spot. The average member saves $28 per shopping trip.

I designed, developed, and launched this app solo — from idea to App Store approval — including the backend, subscription billing, receipt scanner, and the full marketing site.

⚡

Real-time barcode scanner

Tech Stack

How it's built.

Built with Flutter for cross-platform mobile (single codebase for iOS & Android), Firebase for real-time data and auth, ML Kit for the barcode scanner, and a Python backend for price aggregation and receipt OCR.

Flutter Dart Firebase Firestore Firebase Auth ML Kit (Barcode) Cloud Functions Python REST APIs StoreKit / Play Billing RevenueCat OCR / AI Vision Xcode / Android Studio
My Process

From idea to the App Store.

1

Research & design

Identified the price-gap problem through personal experience. Designed all screens in Figma, validated the flow with real shoppers before writing a line of code.

2

Core scanner

Integrated ML Kit barcode scanning with a custom Firebase-backed price database. Optimized to return results in under 2 seconds.

3

Subscriptions & billing

Built in-app purchases through Apple StoreKit and Google Play Billing, managed server-side via RevenueCat for cross-platform receipt validation.

4

Receipt scanner (Pro feature)

Used AI Vision / OCR to parse grocery receipts line-by-line, cross-referenced with the price database, and surfaced total overpayment per trip.

5

Launch & marketing

Submitted to Apple App Store and Google Play. Built the full marketing landing page. Handled App Store Optimization, screenshots, and metadata solo.
